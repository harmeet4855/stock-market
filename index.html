Return

Return is the amount of profit that comes to you as the result of your investment. more info

Agility

You can learn to profit from a stock no matter if it goes up, down, or sideways. That’s very tough to do in business, but in the stock market, it doesn’t matter—because there are profit strategies for movement in any direction.

Liquidity

The stock market offers us the ability to easily buy and sell your paper assets. This is liquidity. Liquidity is the ease with which an asset can be converted to cash.

Share

Shares are rights to assets or securities.A share stands for a share in a company.

Listing

Listing, also known as IPO (Intitial Public Offering) is process of opening the sales of a company's share to the public.

Dividends

Dividend is sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves)

Portfolio

Portfolio is a document where, all the details about your equity investments will be recorded.

Diversification

Diversification is the process of including vastly different companies' share in your portfolio as that when a market dip happens, you can take it.
